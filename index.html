How to Play: Guess Who 'The Simpsons' Edition!

- Each player has a character chosen at random, the goal is to guess your opponent's character before they guess yours! Player 1 is trying to guess the character behind the pink card, Player 2 is the blue one.

- Players take it in turns to ask questions about the character they are trying to guess. Based on the answers given, the player who has asked a question can click on characters on their side of the board to 'eliminate' them!

- Once a player thinks they are ready to make a guess, instead of asking a question, they can input who they think their opponent's character is.

If they are wrong... it is their opponent's turn to ask a question or make a guess. If they are right... they win!
